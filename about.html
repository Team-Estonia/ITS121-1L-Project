Welcome!

History

Founded in 2021 by Jonas and Cherish Ragas, Chef Janno’s was built on a deep passion for quality food and a love for family. The heart of our restaurant is inspired by our very own Chef Janno, whose dedication to great flavors and fresh ingredients drives everything we do.

About Us

Chef Janno's Chill & Grill was born to create gourmet burgers, "Probably the Best Burger in the World." A mission of this magnitude requires an obsession with quality, and it all began with our own Chef Janno.

Mission

Chef Janno’s mission is to serve good quality food—we are not fast food. Your food is freshly cooked upon order.

Special Features

At Chef Janno’s, we serve a diverse menu that blends Western favorites like burgers with well-loved Filipino rice meals and more. We believe that good food brings people together, which is why our restaurant is designed as a welcoming space for everyone—especially families.

We are not fast food. Every dish is freshly cooked upon order, ensuring the best quality and taste in every bite. Chef Janno’s mission is simple: to serve delicious, satisfying meals at an affordable price, making sure that every visit feels like home.

To make your dining experience even more enjoyable, we offer a selection of board games and table games like Jenga and UNO cards. Whether you're bonding with family or having fun with friends, you can make great memories while waiting for your food to be served. At Chef Janno’s, it’s not just about the meal—it’s about the moments shared around the table.
